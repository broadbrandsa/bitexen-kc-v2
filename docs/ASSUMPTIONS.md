# Assumptions

This proposal site presents a **directional strategy**. It is not a committed
plan, a contract, or a validated financial model. The following assumptions
apply to all content on the site.

## No internal data access

- The site was produced **without access to Kaizer Chiefs internal data** —
  CRM, membership, ticketing, app analytics, sponsorship contracts or financials.
- Audience figures quoted in the deck (e.g. *17M supporters*, *75% aged 16–35*)
  are taken from the source presentation and treated as **directional inputs**,
  not verified internal numbers.

## Directional strategy only

- All concepts (token model, revenue streams, experiences, roadmap) are
  **illustrative of the opportunity**, not finalised product or commercial terms.
- The commercial flywheel and value-cycle diagrams describe **mechanisms**, not
  modelled outcomes or guaranteed returns.
- Naming, phasing and feature sets are **placeholders for discussion**.

## Requires validation post-approval

Before any execution, the following must be validated with Kaizer Chiefs and
relevant advisors:

- Regulatory and compliance position for any token / digital asset in-market.
- Integration approach with the in-development Master App and financial services
  strategy.
- Audience, engagement and commercial baselines using real internal data.
- Sponsor appetite and partnership commercial structure.
- Detailed financial modelling and investment case.

## Content provenance

- Narrative and structure are derived from `Kaizer Chiefs_Presentation_v1.docx`.
- Visual design system, lock screen and component library are adapted from the
  existing Kaizer Chiefs proposal codebase (read-and-copy only).
